Think and Grow Rich
Napoleon Hill      Business & Strategy
Think and Grow Rich by Napoleon Hill is a timeless self-help classic that explores the principles behind success and wealth creation. Based on insights from successful leaders like Andrew Carnegie and Henry Ford, Hill outlines a 13-step philosophy that empowers readers to harness the power of their thoughts, set clear goals, and take determined action. This book offers valuable lessons on mindset, personal discipline, and strategic planning, making it a guide for achieving both financial and personal growth.

Evelyn Brooks      Autobiography
A Heavy Lift by Evelyn Brooks is an inspiring autobiography about overcoming life’s toughest challenges. This heartfelt memoir offers readers a powerful reminder of the strength within us all to face adversity and keep going
